Requirements


We recommend all members to be familiar with Korean Terminology as it is frequently used in class. Below is a list of common phrases. The terms represent a composition of basic Korean expressions in the pronounced form only. For a more comprehensive list of words and phrases describing stances, body parts, hand and foot techniques please refer to the attached PDF.

INTRODUCTORY TERMS

  • Tae Kwon Do - The way of hands & feet
  • Charyot - Attention
  • Kyong ye- Bow or Salute
  • Junbi- Ready
  • Si Jak- Begin or start
  • Ba Ro - Return to order
  • Chose- Rest or relax
  • Sabum - Instructor
  • Ki Hap - Yell or Shout
  • Dan - Degree
  • Gup - Level
  • Gam Sa Hap Ni Da - Thank You Very Much
  • Do Jang - Martial Arts Training School
  • Mook Nyum - Meditation
